About

I am currently employed as a software engineer at the Jožef Stefan Institute, where I work on the implementation of the NutriBase nutritional research system. I am also a master's student at the University of Ljubljana, where I research type systems and static analysis for dynamic languages.

My personal work ranges from compiler implementation and emulation to distributed systems, graphics, and web development.
